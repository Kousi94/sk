FAQ’s

What are the best websites for booking flights?
Makemytrip, Cleartrip, Goibibo, Yatra, etc. are some of the best companies to book cheap flight tickets. They offer both domestic and international flight tickets.

Is it safe to use these travel websites?
These websites offer secure transactions where credit card details are stored safely. They keep only basic personal information such as travelers' names, email addresses, and phone numbers. No sensitive financial information is shared with third parties.

Can I buy tickets directly from airline companies?
Yes. You can buy tickets directly from the airlines. However, you won't be able to get lucrative cashback deals and bank offers from the airlines. These offers are usually extended by the travel portals.

What is the cheapest way of traveling in India?
The cheapest mode of transportation in India is the train. Flights can also cost similar to rail journeys, especially for Tier-1 cities, if you book them in advance.

Which is the best site to book adventure activities in India?
You can use websites such as thrillophillia.com, Klook, GetYourGuide, etc. to book adventure sports and activities and plan niche tourism.
